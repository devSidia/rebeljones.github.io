A B O U T

Siblings, Sidia, Kayli, Shaine and Michelle started Knockout Kicks because they loved collecting rare, unique sneakers. There was not easy way to do it and nothing online that allowed them to search and secure those sneakers in a safe way.
That's why Knockout Kicks is the best place to go for the sneaker head fix.Along the way, the siblings have opened up 4 stores across North America. They have stores in Vancouver and Toronto, Canada, Manhattan, New York and Miami, Florida. Their aim is to be the bridge that covers that gap in providing a way for sneakers heads all over the world to keep collecting the shoes they love.
